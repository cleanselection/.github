Selecting text should feel closer to marking a page than wrestling with a tiny browser highlight. Clean Selection lets readers gently paint across words and lines with an airbrush-like gesture, then copy, quote, collect, or erase what they selected.

It is designed for articles, conversations, forums, documentation, and other places where the exact words matter.

## See it in action

- **[Live playground and configurator](https://cleanselection.com/)** — try the selection experience and explore its appearance.
- **[Forum demo](https://cleanselection.com/forum/)** — select a passage from a post or comment and quote it in a reply.
- **[Collect-fragments demo](https://cleanselection.com/multi/)** — gather passages from different parts of a page and copy them together.

## What it enables

- Smooth selection across wrapped lines.
- Mouse and touchscreen interaction.
- Multiple collected excerpts in their original reading order.
- Selection that can be adjusted or erased visually.
- Copying and quoting without changing the typography of the page.

## Open-source project

The [Clean Selection repository](https://github.com/cleanselection/cleanselection) contains the browser library and documentation. An installable WordPress package is available from the [latest release](https://github.com/cleanselection/cleanselection/releases/latest). The core library is available under the MIT License.

Clean Selection also provides the selection experience behind [Re:Likes](https://relikes.com/), a project for reacting to exact passages rather than entire pages.
